Ministry of Science and Higher Education

The Ministry of Science and Higher Education (MoSHE), established by proclamation number 1097/2018 in October 2018, is responsible to lead the development of science, higher education as well as the technical and vocational education and training (TVET) in Ethiopia.

Services

GVS

GVS is a national online document verification system that allows organizations to compare a person's information with a government record so as to assure that the information is not fraud.

Applicant Allocation System

System targeting on solving the time-consuming process of allocating applicants on government sponsored education for masters and PHD from government universities.

Job Portal

A web application for governmental organizations to post Vacancies and collect CV and other pertaining documents from potential applicants. And also it is a platform where users register as applicants can go through vacancy posts, get notified of new vacancies and get detailed information.
