Job Portal

A web application for governmental organizations to post Vacancies and collect CV and other pertaining documents from potential applicants. And also it is a platform where users register as applicants can go through vacancy posts, get notified of new vacancies and get detailed information.

GVS

GVS is a national online document verification system that allows organizations to compare a person's information with a government record so as to assure that the information is not fraud.
